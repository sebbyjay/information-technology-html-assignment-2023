Facilities

Latrobe feature many state of the art facilities close to and inside the campus, ranging from sports, to the arts, and all those in between. favourites of the University include:
Strathallan Golf Club, which as of 2018 has a 5 year lease continued to the University,
Bundoora Park Farm and Cafe, Situated just a few kms outside the campus, home to wildlife and farm animals, and a cafe suitable for small catering jobs.
Indoor Sports Centre, Which houses a Gym, Swimming Pool, Rock Climbing Wall, and Basketball courts among others, a fantastic facility that gives opportunity to stay in shape and have fun,

Food - The Agora

Around Latrobe houses many vending machines, around the campus, providing refreshment and snacks, and are quite common to see, usually not more than 400m between each. The Hub of LaTrobe, known as the agora, is home to many restaurants, some examples include:

  • Mamak Rice and Noodle
  • Thanh Thanh
  • Grain Express
  • Caffeine
  • Bachelor of Coffee
  • Bake n' Bean

Eagle Bar

Latrobe is also home to the eagle bar, a large venue able to cater to 1200 including the outdoor seating, A great choice for large events such as cocktail parties, corporate events, and music performances, and has notable inclusions of a retractable glass canopy, bar, outdoor seating and an open fireplace.
