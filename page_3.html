Travel Options - Public Transport

LaTrobe, being the enormous school that it is, has many public transport lines directly to it from many of melbourne's suburbs, with many modes of transport including bus, train

Cars and Parking

From the northern suburbs, plenty road it your best option, as Latrobe is directly connected to plenty road. The western suburbs may make use of the western ring road, and then onto plenty road, melbourne goer can just head north, and then onto plenty road to get to the Campus.
Latrobe has 11 different car parks, each sections around every side of the campus, with parking passes available online, with different levels/privelages based on the colour of the permit, or individual permits available from the ticket vending machines on each section

Alternate Travel Options

Uber and taxi services are other options for travel if you cannot drive or make use of public transport. Bikes and scooters are also a great option, and source of excersise if you live close to the campus, and bike racks are scattered throughout.
