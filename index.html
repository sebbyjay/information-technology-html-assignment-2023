InfoSysAnalysis Annual General Meeting

InfoSysAnalysis

InfoSysAnalysis is a tech company aimed around data analysis and cybersecurity. InfoSysAnalysis employs cutting edge data scientists and cybersecurity experts in order to discover new and dangerous malware and security threats particularly those who threaten other businesses with data loss and privacy.
This Year InfoSysAnalysis will be holding a meeting on the grounds of LaTrobe University, Alma Mater of the founders of the company, to discuss new investors, company direction and as a fun get together to build bonds between coworkers.

About Us

Founded in 2013, InfoSysAnalysis was the brainchild of Alvin Monk and Don Keedic, Both of which graduated from Latrobe University that same Year. instead of joining an already existing firm, they decided that creating their own business would be their best course of action, giving them full creative control over their business as to grow it based on what they felt was important to them

About the Founders

Alvin Monk took data science whilst Don Keedic was working on his bachelor of cybersecurity, and they both collaborated with each other during their degrees during multiple classes to streamline their way to acheiving their degrees.
