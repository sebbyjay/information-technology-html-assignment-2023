La Trobe University Campus

Latrobe holds many building for students to work in, for meetings to take place and also holds many local modern art installations to admire during your stay at LaTrobe. The urban planning of the campus makes it easy to get to anywhere from anywhere, from its concretes paths and central location of the agora, streamlining travel inside the campus.

Conference Location

LaTrobe University is home to many Gardens and outdoor spaces, with the environment being a key part of latrobe culture. Some to note are:

The Simpson Lawn, a large grass area surrounded by trees and a moat, suitable for large functions and events;
The Academic Lawn, by the lake at la trobe, close to the eagle, which poses as a nice quiet space to study at and relax, with the sounds of the water and wildlife at your ears and;
The International Gardens, close to the Agora, a "picturesque venue for any outdoor event"
LaTrobe also has a community garden where anyone can grow fruits and vegetables as a sustainable practice, and teaches gardening skills such as composting, planting, and mulching.
